Terms of Service & Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. Cashless Payment Policy

GetMe-Ryde LLC operates as a strictly cashless transportation dispatch platform. All payments, fees, and tips must be securely settled using a valid credit card or debit card via our electronic terminal systems or booking software.

Drivers and independent contractors are instructed not to accept cash under any circumstances. By booking a ride, you agree to provide a valid payment method authorized for the full estimated fare.

2. SMS & Communication Consent

By providing your mobile phone number and checking the consent box on our dispatch forms, you explicitly agree to receive automated and manual text messages (SMS) from GetMe-Ryde LLC regarding ride updates, driver dispatch notifications, and administrative inquiries.

Opt-Out: You may cancel or revoke this consent at any time. To stop receiving text messages, simply text "STOP" in reply to any message received, call us at +1 (877) 766-8002, or email our support desk at support@getme-ryde.com.

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier plan. Frequency of messages varies based on your active booking status.

3. Privacy Policy & Data Sharing

Your privacy is paramount to us. GetMe-Ryde LLC collects information such as your full name, email address, phone number, and location details strictly to coordinate precise local taxi services and airport transfers.

No Third-Party Sharing: We do not sell, rent, share, or disclose your personal information or mobile data to third-party marketers or affiliates for commercial purposes. All data remains confidential within our protected dispatch logs.

4. Limitation of Liability

GetMe-Ryde acts as a dispatch management entity connecting passengers with professional local transit lines. While we enforce strict fleet quality standards, GetMe-Ryde LLC is not responsible for unexpected flight delays, traffic conditions, or personal items misplaced inside independent fleet vehicles.
